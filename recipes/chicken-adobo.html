Chicken Adobo


Ingredients

  • 2 bls. Chicken Thighs
  • 1 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 cup Cane Viniger
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 3-4 bay leaf
  • 4 TBSP blackpeper corns
  • 1 Onion sliced
  • 8 colves Garlic minced

Process

  1. In a 5-8qt dutch oven or pot, brown chicken thighs on medium-high heat
  2. After about 5-10 minutes on each side or until chicken is thuroughly browned on both sides remove from pot and set aside
  3. in the same pot cook onions until translucent about 5 minutes stir occasionally
  4. after about 5 minutes add garlic and cook for another 2-3 minutes stir occasionally to keep garlic from burning
  5. add blackpepper and cook another minute while stirring
  6. add chicken back into the pot and pour in soy sauce, viniger and coconut milk
  7. bring to a boil then bring donw heat to a simmer
  8. add in bay leaves, cover pot and let simmer on low for 45mins to an hour
  9. after and hour it should be nice and tender and ready to serve with rice
