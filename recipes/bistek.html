Bistek Tagalog

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chuck roast beef thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion sliced into rings
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 2 TBSP black pepper
  • 1 TBSP brown sugar
  • 3 bay leaves

Process

  1. in a wok, begin searing the beef on medium-high heat
  2. once thuroughly browned add in your onion slices and stir aroud to cook
  3. then add in your black pepper and cook until onions are softened
  4. add in your soy sauce, lemon juice and brown sugar
  5. add bay leaves
  6. bring to a simmer and cover for 20-30 min on low
  7. it is done when beef is tender to your liking
  8. serve with rice
