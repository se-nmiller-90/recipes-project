Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. Beef cut into 1 in. cubes
  • 1 large potato cubed
  • 1 carrot sliced thick
  • 1 onion thinly sliced
  • 8 cloves garlic minced
  • 1.5 cups peas
  • 2 TBSP tomato paste
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 TBSP beef bullion
  • 3 bay leaves

Process

  1. sear beef cubes on medium-high heat in a large pot
  2. remove beef and add onion slices and cook until translucent
  3. add garlic and stir for a minute or two then add tomato paste
  4. add in carrots and potatoes and cover for about a minute
  5. then add back in the beef cubes with the cans of crushed tomatoes
  6. stir all together and add beef bullion and bay leaves
  7. cover and let simmer on low for an hour stiring occasionally
  8. add salt and pepper to taste
  9. water can be added if a thinner consistancy is desired
